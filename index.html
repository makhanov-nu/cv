About Me

Bridging AI & Healthcare

I am a researcher with a PhD in Computer Science from Nazarbayev University. My work focuses on applying Federated Learning to medical image classification, enabling privacy-preserving AI in healthcare.

Currently a Visiting Scholar at Queen's University Belfast and Senior Research Scientist at Astana IT University, I explore the intersection of Business, Healthcare, and Artificial Intelligence.

Beyond research, I have extensive teaching experience and a passion for mentoring the next generation of AI researchers.
